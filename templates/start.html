USE CASES

home page

click account on home page to login or sign up



example user account

user can click volunteer on home page to search and apply for volunteering

user can edit their account, view purchase or volunteering history

users can click exchange on the home page in order to go through the ads posted and purchase them



example organization account

organizations can click recruit on the home page to post their opportunities

organizations can edit their account, view their post history in order to approve applicants



example vendor account

vendors can advertise their products by clicking advertise on home page

vendors can edit their account or view their post history. They will be able to see how many sales they made in their post history.
